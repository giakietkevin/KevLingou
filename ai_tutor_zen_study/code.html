Grammar Insight

JLPT N4 open_in_new

〜で (Location of Action)

Indicates the place where an action occurs. In your sentence "図書館で", it marks the library as the place of study.

Ex: 公園で走ります。
V-Past open_in_new

〜ました (Polite Past)

The polite past tense form of verbs. Used for completed actions in the past.

Ex: 食べます → 食べました
